The Career of Johnny Depp
Analysis: For the top two charts I decided to make a radar chart and a polar bar chart. For the radar chart on the left I looked at what the top 7 genres of movies Depp acts in and saw that drama takes the lead. I then wanted to see what are the average ratings of the movies in those categories and made the polar bar chart on the right. By observing this chart we can see that even though he mainly acts in dramas, those tend to be his lowest rated movies while his documentaries are the highest rated. Each point/slice can be hovered over to give a detailed view of the counts of each movie in the genres.
Analysis: For the top two charts I decided to make a bubble chart and heatmap focusing on Depp's Filmogrophy. On the left I made a bubble chart depicting a timeline of different projects and showed how they rated by their filmogrophy type. The size of the bubble is determined by the number of ratings the filmogrophy got. Each bubble can be hovered over to give info on the title of the film, the exact rating, the number of ratings, and its year created. We can see that movies dominate the majority of his filmogrophy and it (and other categories) can be toggled on/off by clicking on the corresponding bubble on legend. On the right I created a heat map of Depp's filmogrophy over the years adn the numbers of ratings they received. As the map shows, the filmogrophy that Depp participated in has an average rating between 6.5 of 7.4. We can also see that Depp has been most active mainly since 2000 with a high period between 2005 - 2009. Grids can be hovered over for more information and the right tab shows a bar chart of the number of films for a specific rating. (Note: A few TV series were omitted from both charts because they started before Depp's career)
Analysis: For this chart I wanted to plot some of Johnny Depp's biggest movies, The Pirates of the Caribbean, and see how much they grossed in various countries. Using data from boxofficemojo.com, I managed to clean it and plot it on a world map. Here we can see some interesting things. After the second movie the series made less money in North America but more in other parts of the world like China and Russia. It is also interesting to note that all the movies in the series were played in China except for the second one. This is because the second movie was censored in China and never displayed there and therefore never made any money.
